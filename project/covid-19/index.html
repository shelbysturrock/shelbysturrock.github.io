COVID-19

Apr 27, 2016
Shelby L. Sturrock
PhD Candidate in Epidemiology at the University of Toronto

I am a PhD candidate in epidemiology using consumer wearable technology to measure to impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on physical activity. I also serve as data lead for the COVID-19 Canada Open Data Working Group.
